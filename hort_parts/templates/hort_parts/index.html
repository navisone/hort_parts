About US

Trademark HORT, founded by the German company M-Tech Autoteile GmbH, appeared on the market of auto components in 2009, introducing cooling pumps for passenger cars. At present TM HORT offers to motorists and service specialists a significant list of spare parts, which include:

  • Shock absorbers
  • Clutch elements
  • Brake pads and cylinders
  • Thermostats
  • Cooling pumps
  • Starters
  • Generators
  • High-voltage wires
  • Timing belts
  • Drive belts
  • Gas stops
  • Constantly done painstaking work to expand the range of products

TM HORT in the production of products uses the latest automated control systems for quality control and shipment of products in accordance with the requirements of ISO / TS 16949, which allows you to track all the stages of planning, preparation and operation of production, tooling, finished products.
This approach makes it possible to ensure the quality of TM HORT products and assume warranty obligations to the consumer for at least 12 months of their operation. TM HORT makes maximum efforts to promote quality auto components to the secondary market.
